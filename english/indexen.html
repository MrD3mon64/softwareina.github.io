Who We Are?

We are a secondary education institution with over 40 years of experience in shaping high-quality graduates committed to society.

The Instituto Nacional de Apopa was founded on February 7, 1981, at the location of Escuela Vicente Acosta. A classroom was used to run a 1st-year high school section with 44 students.

The individuals responsible for the operation of this new institution in its first year were: Professor José Jerónimo Yánez (Director), Professor Rubén Alirio Gomero Canjura (Q.D.D.G), Mrs. María Ramos (Secretary), and Mr. Angelino Herrera Salazar (Orderly). Thus, the institution was born, and today it has become an Institute of Educational Innovation, enjoying national prestige and recognition for the quality of graduates who leave each year, as well as for the excellent performance of teachers and administrative staff.

At that time, only Academic High School was offered. However, in 1984, the land where the institution currently operates was acquired, and the construction of classrooms began gradually, with the support and efforts of parents and the educational community. New study options were expanded, so by 1988, the institution offered: Academic High School, Commercial High School, and Health High School.

By that time, there were around 1,000 students attended by 30 teachers. The desire for improvement has always been the driving force behind the search for new options to provide more opportunities for students to develop their full potential and effectively integrate into society, the labor market, or start their own businesses.

With this vision, a systematic approach was promoted to involve students in artistic, sports, and cultural activities, which has resulted in positive outcomes and national and international recognition.

Due to the efforts of teachers, parents, and students in general, the Instituto Nacional de Apopa now has 68 teachers and 26 administrative staff to serve 1,681 students. It has been privileged with inclusion in relevant national educational projects such as the CRA Project (Center for Learning Resources) and the APREMAT Project (Support for the Process of Reforming Secondary Education in the Technical Area).

Since 2015, the Revised Curriculum has been developed, offering Vocational Technical Administrative Accounting High School, Primary Health Care, Electronics, Software Creation, Daytime General High School, General High School, and Technical Vocational High School in Commerce and Administration with options in Accounting and Secretarial Night School, and Flexible General High School on Saturdays. Additionally, the General High School includes a diploma in Tourist Operations Technician, and the Technical programs include a diploma in Entrepreneurship.

SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT

3 YEARS

You will gain knowledge that allows you to enter the world of cybernetics with all technological advances in the industrial, commercial, and service sectors, acquiring expertise in:

Programming and design of information systems, application of multimedia designs, development of quality system manuals for software development, web pages,knowledge of entrepreneurship techniques, information systems, and software creation of micro-enterprises through cooperatives, among others.

This High School program will prepare you to integrate into software development processes with extensive knowledge and skills in the technological field, with the ability to work in teams and engage in entrepreneurship. With this training, the graduate will be able to work in software development companies, institutions, and organizations that implement software in specific technology centers, depending on the characteristics of the companies..

With a Technical High School degree in Software Development, you can work in the following areas:

-Software solutions sales

-Application maintenance support

-Software development training

-Quality control

You can also work in the following technological fields:

1- Cloud Computing (Development of applications running from the web)

2- Development of components for Web 2.0 or 3.0

3- Tools for generating code for software solutions
